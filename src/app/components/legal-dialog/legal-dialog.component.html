Data Privacy & Security

security Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using browser technologies:

  • Primary Storage: IndexedDB for reliable, structured data storage
  • Backup Storage: localStorage for data persistence and fallback
  • No Server Communication: Your data never leaves your device
  • No Tracking: We do not collect, transmit, or analyze your personal data

cloud_off No External Data Transmission

This application operates entirely offline after initial loading:

  • Friend information and photos are stored locally
  • Event data remains on your device
  • No analytics or telemetry data is collected
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

person User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download your complete data at any time
  • Import: Restore data from previous exports
  • Delete: Clear all data through the settings dialog
  • Modify: Edit or remove any friends or events

photo Photo URLs

Regarding profile photos:

  • Photo URLs you provide are stored locally and used only for display
  • The app includes a random photo generator using randomuser.me API
  • External photo URLs are loaded directly by your browser
  • No photo data is processed or stored by our application

browser Browser Data

Standard browser storage mechanisms used:

  • IndexedDB: Structured data storage (friends, events)
  • localStorage: Backup and settings storage
  • sessionStorage: Not used
  • Cookies: Not used for data storage
